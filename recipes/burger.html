Odin's Tex-mex Burger

Description

This Tex-Mex burger is expertly crafted with an irresistable Cajun mayo that yields a flavor never tasted until today. The beef is a perfect mix with the jazzy and tangy mayo, it's a mystery why it hasn't been used until today!

This is no ordinary, run of the mill burger, it's a personal recipe handed down many generations and is said to have been used by Odin. Whether you belive this tale, it's still one the greatest burgers to have grazed This mortal plane! Enough talking, let's get cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1/2
  • 4 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, divided*Odin's favorite!*
  • 1 1/3 pounds ground beef sirloin
  • 1/2 cup diced white onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce*Can be removed*
  • 4 slices pepper Jack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Combine ground sirloin, oinion, remaining 3 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, jalapeno, garlic, and Wrocestershire sauce in a large bowl. Divide into 4 balls; flatten into patties.
  4. Cook on the preheated grill until burgers are no longer pink in center, about 5 minutes per side, topping each burger with 1 cheese slice, during the last 2 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into centers should read at least 160 *F (70 degrees C).
  5. Spread mayo on buns, add burgers, lettuce, and tomatoes.
