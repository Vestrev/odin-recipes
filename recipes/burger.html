Odin's Tex-mex Burger

Description

This Tex-Mex burger is expertly crafted with an irresistable Cajun mayo that yields a flavor never tasted until today. The beef is a perfect mix with the jazzy and tangy mayo, it's a mystery why it hasn't been used until today!

This is no ordinary, run of the mill burger, it's a personal recipe handed down many generations and is said to have been used by Odin. Whether you belive this tale, it's still one the greatest burgers to have grazed This mortal plane! Enough talking, let's get cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1/2
  • 4 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, divided

    Odin's favorite

  • 1 1/3 pounds ground beef sirloin
  • 1/2 cup diced white onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

    *Can be removed*

  • 4 slices pepper Jack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato
