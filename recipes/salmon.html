Maple Salmon Recipe

Description

This is maple salmon recipe that is restaurant grade! I am sure that this meal with keep you salivating from desire. this recipe is based on a recipe that my grandmother provided to me. It is built on a maple syrup foundation.

Get ready for a really simple, but sweet and savory meal! Once you finish, I would love a picture of how it all looks. Without further ado! These are the ingredients required!

Ingredients:

  • Maple syrup
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning
  • Salmon

Steps:

  1. Chop salmon into fillets, make the marinade
  2. Marinate fillets in the fridge for at least 30 minutes recommended: 1hr 30m
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the fish flakes easily
